## What to Expect as a Participant

If you are eligible and choose to enroll, your participation will include two main components over the course of the study. 

---

### 1. Four Laboratory Visits (Within 1 Week)

You will visit our research lab on the MU campus in Columbia, MO to complete computerized learning tasks. During these tasks, you will view various visual stimuli, some of which predict the receipt of a liquid reward (a fruit juice of your choice, up to 8 ounces total). While you complete these tasks, an fMRI scan will look at how your brain processes these visual cues and rewards.

#### Laboratory Pay Breakdown
You are paid in cash at the end of **every single visit**. Your payment includes a base hourly rate ($20/hour), a daily travel allowance, and a daily incentive for arriving on time.

* **Base Visit Pay:** Earned for the duration of each session:
  * Visit 1 (1 hour): $20
  * Visit 2 (2 hours): $40
  * Visit 3 (1.5 hours): $30
  * Visit 4 (1.5 hours): $30
* **Daily Travel Allowance:** You receive $10 for **every single day** you come to the lab to help cover gas and transportation.
* **Daily On-Time Incentive:** You receive an additional $10 for **every single day** you are in the lobby by your scheduled start time.
* **Final Completion Bonus:** You receive an additional $60 bonus at your final visit for completing the full 4-day laboratory protocol.

By arriving on time **every single day** and completing all sessions, your total laboratory earnings will be **$260**.

---

### 2. Five Online Surveys (Over 1 Year)

You will complete five remote, online research surveys. The first is completed between your first and second lab visits. The remaining four are follow-up surveys completed 3, 6, 9, and 12 months after your final lab visit. All survey compensation is distributed via electronic gift cards.

#### Survey Pay Breakdown
Each survey takes approximately 15 minutes to complete. 

* **Base Survey Pay:** You receive $10 for each completed survey.
* **On-Time Incentive:** You receive an additional $10 for each survey completed within 48 hours of receiving the email prompt.
* **Survey Milestone Bonus:** You receive an additional $30 bonus for completing the final 12-month survey.

By completing all five surveys on time, your total survey earnings will be **$130**.

---

### Total Potential Compensation

By completing the entire study and earning all daily on-time incentives, your total compensation is **$390**. 

As a token of appreciation, participants who complete all laboratory visits may also request a digital image of their structural MRI scan to keep as a personal memento.
