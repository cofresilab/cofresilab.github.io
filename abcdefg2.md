### 1. Four Laboratory Visits (Within 1 Week)

You will visit our research lab on the MU campus in Columbia, MO to complete computerized learning tasks. During these tasks, you will view various visual stimuli, some of which predict the receipt of a liquid reward (a fruit juice of your choice, up to 8 ounces total). [cite_start]Your functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) recording will be collected so that we can visualize how your brain processes these visual stimuli and liquid rewards[cite: 1, 2].

#### Laboratory Pay Breakdown
[cite_start]You are paid in cash at the end of **every single visit**[cite: 1, 7]. [cite_start]Your payment includes a base hourly rate ($20/hour) [cite: 1, 14][cite_start], a daily travel allowance [cite: 1, 10][cite_start], and a daily incentive for arriving on time[cite: 1, 9]. 

| Laboratory Visit | Base Visit Pay | Daily On-Time Incentive | Daily Travel Allowance | Completion Bonus | Total Cash Paid End of Visit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Visit 1** (1 hour) | $20 | $10 | $10 | — | **$40** |
| **Visit 2** (2 hours) | $40 | $10 | $10 | — | **$60** |
| **Visit 3** (1.5 hours) | $30 | $10 | $10 | — | **$50** |
| **Visit 4** (1.5 hours) | $30 | $10 | $10 | $60 | **$110** |
| **Total Laboratory Earnings** | | | | | **$260** |

* [cite_start]**Daily On-Time Incentive:** To earn this extra $10 for **every single day**, you must be physically present in the lobby by your scheduled start time[cite: 1, 9].
* [cite_start]**Daily Travel Allowance:** You receive $10 for **every single day** you come to the lab to help cover gas and transportation[cite: 1, 10].
* [cite_start]**Final Completion Bonus:** An additional $60 bonus is added to your final visit payment for completing the full 4-day protocol[cite: 1, 8].

[cite_start]You will complete five remote, online research surveys[cite: 1, 2, 16]. [cite_start]The first is completed between your first and second lab visits[cite: 1, 16]. [cite_start]The remaining four are longitudinal research surveys completed 3, 6, 9, and 12 months after your final lab visit[cite: 1, 21].
