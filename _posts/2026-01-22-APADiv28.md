Dr. Cofresí has been selected as this year’s recipient of the Division 28 Young Psychopharmacologist Award. The Young Psychopharmacologist Award honors a young scientist doing original, meritorious work in psychopharmacology and encourages excellence in research at the interface between the disciplines of pharmacology and psychology. Congratulations!!
